The brand: a proud
legacy since 1998

Today, Bembi stands as Ukraine's largest brand of children's clothing, specializing in apparel for children aged 0 to 13 years. The brand proudly represents Ukrainian-made quality, embodying the motto: “Bembi - Care For Kids.”

Rooted in values of responsibility, honesty, and a relentless pursuit of growth and innovation, Bembi fosters strong, positive relationships with its partners, consumers, and employees.

With a network of 30 branded stores and a regional distribution network of 700 sales points across Ukraine, Bembi continues to expand its reach. The brand is constantly evolving, exploring new markets, and staying ahead of global trends in children's fashion. In 2015, Bembi established a representative office in Europe—Children's World, located in Warsaw, Poland.

Today, Children's World Sp. z o.o. is the exclusive distributor representing Bembi across the Polish and European markets. The company has established a strong customer base in numerous countries, including Poland, Czech Republic, Slovenia, Estonia, Latvia, Lithuania, Hungary, Romania, UK, Belgium, Germany, Switzerland, France, Sweden, Austria, and the Netherlands.
